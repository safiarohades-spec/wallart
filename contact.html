Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I print my digital files?
    You can print them at home using a high-quality color printer, upload them to an online printing service, or take the files on a USB to a local print shop (like Staples, FedEx, or local photo labs) for professional results.
  • What paper should I use?
    For the best aesthetic finish, we highly recommend printing on heavyweight matte paper, premium cardstock, or fine art textured paper rather than standard glossy photo paper.
